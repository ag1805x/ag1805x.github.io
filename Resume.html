  • Dec - Apr 2017 : M.Sc. dissertation. Alagappa University, Karaikudi, TN, India
    Thesis title: In vitro and in silico studies on Kanamycin resistance protein (TTHA1575) from Thermus thermophilus HB8
    Purification of the target protein was carried out by cloning and over-expressing the gene construct through E. Coli.
    Used molecular modelling approach to understand the structure and function of the target protein.

    Thesis title: Study of the bacterial diversity in seawater of Gujarat coast using culture independent (metagenomics) approach
    Used gene targeted metagenomics approach to infer the bacterial diversity at three sites.
  • Jan - May 2015 : B.Sc. dissertation. Institute of Genetic Engineering, Kolkata, WB, India
    Thesis title: A computational approach to study the binding of calcitonin peptide family receptors
    Used molecular docking and dynamics to identify the binding modes of calcitonin to its receptors.
